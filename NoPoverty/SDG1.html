SDG 1: No Poverty

Together, we can eradicate poverty and create a better world for all.

What is No Poverty?

Goal 1 aims to eradicate extreme poverty for all people everywhere and reduce inequalities by ensuring access to basic needs such as food, shelter, and education.

Global Poverty Statistics

  • 10% of the world's population lives on less than $2.15 a day.
  • 736 million people still live in extreme poverty.
  • Every child deserves access to education, healthcare, and food.

What Can You Do?

Here are some ways to help fight poverty:

  • Donate to trusted charities.
  • Volunteer in community programs.
  • Spread awareness about poverty and its effects.
